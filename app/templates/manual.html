Memory lvls meaning:
"-" - means that question was not answered yet at all
"0" - means that question was answered but last answer was bad
"1" - means that question was answered and last answer was correct, but it was only one or previous was bad
"2" - means that question was answered correct twice in row, (note that before it was answered incorrect or not at all)
"n" - means that question was answered correct n times in row, (note that before it was answered incorrect or not at all)
